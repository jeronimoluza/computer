# Q&A Flows

Principle: ask one question at a time. Stop early when the section is "good enough" for the
current purpose.

## Mode: Create New Doc
1) What is the working title (and short slug, if you have one)?
2) Who is this for, and what will it be used to decide?
3) One-sentence problem statement?
4) What does success look like (outputs + metrics if any)?
5) What are the primary data sources (URLs/paths) and access constraints?
6) What is the high-level methodology (3-7 steps)?

## Mode: Improve a Section
1) Pick a section and target depth: outline / draft prose / final polish?
2) What is missing or unclear today?
3) What is the acceptance check for this section (done means...)?
4) What references/links must be included?

## Mode: Update References / Links
1) Add/update/remove?
2) Canonical URL/path and access method (public/auth/local)?
3) Any citation format preferences (or keep it simple)?

## Mode: Review/Prioritize TODO
1) Choose a lens: speed / impact / risk / deadline.
2) Any hard dependencies or sequencing?

## Mode: Export
1) Export format: pdf / docx / both?
2) Any constraints (paper size, template, citations), or default?

## Mode: Restore/Recap
1) What do you want to restore: a section, a decision, or the whole state?
2) What is the timeframe (last session vs last week vs all time)?
